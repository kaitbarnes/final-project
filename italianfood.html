Lasagna Recipe


Servings: Literally 8-9 pounds of lasagna
Total Time to Cook: 48 hours

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (not pre-cooked)
  • One pound of ground beef
  • One pound of ground sausage OR veal
  • One pound of ground pork
  • One large yellow onion
  • One big carrot OR equivalent # of baby carrots
  • Two heads of garlic
  • One small package of fresh basil leaves
  • Five bay leaves
  • Dried Sage
  • Two cans of San Marzano tomatoes (whole and peeled)
  • Two cups of chicken stock
  • One and a half cups of milk
  • Half a cup of heavy cream
  • Three ounces of parmesan cheese for sauce, additional for assembly
  • One small container of ricotta cheese
  • Low moisture mozzarella cheese (do not buy pre-grated)

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook down meat and drain juices - remove from the pot.
  2. Dice carrots and onions and cook in a large pot. Season well with oregano and red pepper.
  3. Once vegetables are soft, add two heads of pressed garlic.
  4. Pour in red wine to deglaze vegetables. Pro tip: Use a dry red wine and drink a lot of it.
  5. Add two cups of chicken stock to pot.
  6. Add meat back to pot.
  7. Add two cans of San Marzano tomatoes, half of basil leaves, sage, and bay leaves to mix. you will need to remove the bay leaves and basil after cooking, ensure they are intact.
  8. Add milk to pot.
  9. Let the pot simmer for four hours, partially covered. Stir occasionally.
  10. After four hours, remove bay leaves and basil, and then add half a cup of heavy cream, chopped basil (remainder), and parmesan cheese.
  11. Melt in, add salt and pepper.
  12. Remove sauce and let it sit overnight in the fridge.
  13. The next day, assemble lasagna with sauce on the bottom and top. Add mozzarella cheese to top of lasagna once assembled.
How to assemble:
  1. Cook lasagna noodles, rinse with cold water immediately after to cool them down grate mozzarella cheese and additional parmesan.
  2. Apply sauce to bottom of pan, then alternate noodles, sauce, cheese, noodles until pan is full. make sure to have sauce on top layer of lasagna.
  3. Bake lasagna for one hour at 375 degrees.
  4. When lasagna is done, let rest for 30 minutes and enjoy!

Kyleâ€™s Special Chunky Tomato Sauce

Servings: Around a pound of sauce
Total time to cook: One hour

Ingredients

  • One can of peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • Three red bell peppers
  • Three heads of garlic
  • Two black garlic cloves
  • One onion
  • Two tablespoons of butter
Cooking Instructions
  1. Roast heads of non-black garlic for 10 minutes at 450 degrees in oven.
  2. Add bell peppers to oven (sliced in half with seeds removed) and bake for an additional 40 minutes.
  3. Once finished, remove charred skin of bell peppers and remove roasted garlic cloves from the heads.
  4. Put bell peppers, roasted garlic, and black garlic into food processor or blender and blend until relatively smooth.
  5. Saute onion until soft and transparent and add to food processor. Blend until smooth.
  6. Add blended mix and can of tomatoes to pot and cook on stove while breaking up tomatoes. Bring to a boil and lower to reduce heat and simmer, add two tablespoons of butter or some heavy cream before removing from heat.
  7. Add to any pasta or pizza and enjoy. This sauce will keep in fridge for a maximum of three days.
