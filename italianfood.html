Lasagna Recipe


Servings: Literally 8-9 pounds of lasagna
Total Time to Cook: 48 hours

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • One pound of ground beef
  • One pound of ground sausage OR veal
  • One pound of ground pork
  • One onion
  • One big carrot OR three baby carrots
  • Two heads of garlic
  • Three fresh basil leaves
  • Two bay leaves
  • Two sage leaves
  • Two cans of San Marzano tomatoes (whole and peeled
  • Two cups of chicken stock
  • One and a half cups of milk
  • Half a cup of heavy cream
  • Three ounces of parmesan cheese
  • Two ounces of ricotta cheese
  • A bag of shredded or diced mozzarella cheese

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook down meat and drain juices
  2. Dice carrots and onions and cook in a large pot. Season well with oregano and red pepper
  3. Once vegetables are soft, add two heads of pressed garlic
  4. Pour in red wine to deglaze vegetables. Pro tip: Use Malbec wine and drink a lot of it
  5. Add two cups of chicken stock to pot with vegetables and garlic
  6. Add meat to pot
  7. Add two cans of San Marzano tomatoes, basil leaves, sage leaves, and bay leaves to mix
  8. Add milk to pot
  9. Let the pot simmer for four hours, mostly uncovered. Stir occasionally
  10. After four hours, remove bay leaves and then add half a cup of heavy cream, chopped basil, and parmesan cheese
  11. Melt in, add salt and pepper
  12. Remove sauce and let it sit overnight in the fridge
  13. The next day, assemble lasagna with sauce on the bottom and top. Add mozzarella cheese to top of lasagna once assembled.
  14. Bake lasagna for one hour at 375 degrees
  15. When lasagna is done, let rest for 30 minutes and enjoy!

Kyleâ€™s Special Chunky Tomato Sauce

Servings: Around a pound of sauce
Total time to cook: One hour

Ingredients

  • One can of peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • Three red bell peppers
  • Three heads of garlic
  • Two black garlic cloves
  • One onion
  • Two tablespoons of butter
Cooking Instructions
  1. Roast heads of non-black garlic for 10 minutes at 450 degrees in oven
  2. Add bell peppers to oven and bake for an additional 40 minutes
  3. Once finished, slice up bell peppers
  4. Put sliced bell peppers, roasted garlic, and black garlic into food processor or blender and blend until relatively smooth
  5. Saute onion until soft and transparent and add to food processor. Blend until smooth
  6. Add blended mix and can of tomatoes to pot and cook on stove while breaking up tomatoes. Cook until simmering and add two tablespoons of butter
  7. Add to any pasta or pizza and enjoy. This sauce will keep in fridge for a maximum of three days.
